Community Safety Tips

  • Secure Your Home: Always lock doors and windows, install motion-sensor lights, and consider a home security system.
  • Emergency Contacts: Keep local police and emergency numbers handy. Save them on your phone and in visible areas at home.
  • Stay Alert: Be aware of your surroundings, especially at night or in less populated areas.
  • Walk in Groups: Whenever possible, walk with others and avoid isolated paths.
  • Safe Transportation: Use trusted transportation options and share your travel plans with family or friends.
  • Report Suspicious Activity: Use the C.A.R.E platform to report crimes anonymously and in real-time.
  • Neighborhood Watch: Engage with your community and participate in local safety programs.
  • Know Your Community: Stay informed about local incidents and patterns to make safer decisions.
  • Protect Personal Information: Avoid sharing sensitive details online that could compromise your safety.
  • Social Media Awareness: Be cautious about posting your location or travel plans publicly.
