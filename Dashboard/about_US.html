About C.A.R.E

C.A.R.E, which stands for Community Alert and Response Emergency, is a cutting-edge web application designed to enhance community safety and engagement.
Our platform empowers users to report crimes anonymously, access real-time crime data, and explore community safety scores.
By fostering collaboration between residents and law enforcement, C.A.R.E aims to create safer neighborhoods and promote a sense of security for all.

Key Features

Real-Time Crime Reporting

Report crimes as they happen and help keep your community safe.

Community Safety Scores

Get insights into the safety of different neighborhoods before you move.

Anonymous Reporting

Your identity is protected when you report a crime.

Safety Tips and Resources

Access valuable tips and resources to stay safe in your community.

Police Integration

Work directly with local law enforcement to address community concerns.

Real-Time Community Issues

Get visual insights on real-time community issues.
